About

A poster is an art form. It's design with a dash of creativity against a flavour of subtlety.

Hand-drawn Art

The hand is man's biggest tool. We use it let our imaginations flow out on paper (or digital paper). We use different mediums - water color, pencils, charcoals, even digital pens to draw posters.

HD Photography

Nature, Outdoors, Indoors - if you look around, the world never seizes to intrigue us. We capture the moments that matter and frame them forever. Their right place is on your living room walls.

HQ Poster Rendering

We add 2 with 2 and produce 5. A mix and blend of art, design and media produces just the right poster that will strike a chord. You can connect in a moment and your heart will know 'that's it' !

Story

As we step out of our beds each morning there awaits endless possibilities for us.
Our choices define us. It's time we express them. Let's make way for powerful posters. Let's connect.

  • December 2014

    Our Humble Beginnings

    Great discussions often start over coffee, ours started over wine! Gazing into a starry night in December wondering about the wilderness of the universe, we come up with an idea: Let's make posters talk.

  • January 2015

    Breaking the Ice

    At our very own backyard, we made our very first set of poster, simple yet powerful one. We had broken the ice. It was time to break the iceberg now!

  • March 2015

    A Studio is Born

    From ametuer to experts - it was time to make the transition and tell the world we had arrived! PosterLah! Studio was born to make an impact with powerful posters bound to strike a chord.

  • Today, Now

    A Step into the Future of Posters

    Comprising of expert photographers, editors, content writers, developers, we vouch to take a step into the future of poster-making. Join us and let's grow together.

Captain Jack Sparrow

An excellent poster you'll fall in love with.


This poster was first transformed using gradient map and curves adjustment. Splash of water colors were linear burnt with a mask. A canvas background was multiplied with a gaussian grit feel and vignette.
Feel free to contact us if you need such a poster for your favourite actor or superhero!

Steve Jobs Quote

Inspiring poster to break barriers and push your limits.


This poster was made by gradient mapping and curves adjustment followed by a mask introduction. Later selective masking was done to introduce the quote and other text. Finally the tone was adjusted to produce a classy poster that must be hung in your workplace. Do let us know if you are interested to get similar posters of your role model.

Singapore Map SG50

Vibrant poster of Singapore Map with water color splash.


This is a vibrant poster of a Singapore Map alligned with the SG50 theme. The map is shown using water color splash on a light background. This was done by multiplying and overlaying splashes with a mask of the map. As Singapore embarks it's journey into the 50th year, this is the poster of the season!
